Our Rates

  • Standard Delivery
    • Local Delivery: R105-R755 for a single box or parcel, depending on weight and size
    • National Delivery: R90-R200 for small parcels, R110-R130 for large parcels, with delivery times ranging from 1-7 business days
  • Express Delivery
    • Same Day Express: R555-R755 for urgent shipments, with delivery within 90 minutes of collection
    • Overnight Courier: R105-R130 for packages up to 2kg, with delivery within 1-2 business days
  • Economy Delivery
    • Economy Road: R90-R200 for packages up to 25kg, with delivery within 2-3 business days

Additional costs may apply for:

  • Fuel surcharges: 34.82% of the base charge
  • Waybill surcharges: R5.50
  • VAT: 15% of the subtotal
  • Extra kg or box charges: R50-R95 per kg or box, depending on the service
